Our Services

We support construction clients with BIM-led project insight, stronger controls, and clear analysis across cost, schedule, and risk. Our services are designed for developers, contractors, project teams, and legal advisors who need better visibility, stronger documentation, and more confidence in complex project decisions.

4D / 5D BIM Planning

We develop BIM-based planning models that connect scope, schedule, and cost into a clear visual and analytical framework. This allows project teams to understand sequencing, phasing, dependencies, and financial implications before issues escalate in the field.

Typical scope includes: 4D schedule integration, 5D cost linking, phasing analysis, milestone tracking.

Project Controls

We help clients improve project oversight through structured controls, clearer reporting, and better visibility into actual performance. The focus is not just tracking activity, but creating a reliable basis for decision-making across project delivery.

Typical scope includes: Progress reporting, earned value, risk registers, change management frameworks.

Delay & Disruption Analysis

We analyze how and where project delays occur, what drives them, and how they affect delivery. Our work supports both proactive project management and formal dispute-related review when needed.

Typical scope includes: Delay cause analysis, critical path review, impacted programme, concurrent delay assessment.

Cost Transparency & Forensic Support

We provide structured financial and project-based analysis to clarify where money is being spent, where control may be weakening, and where risk exposure is increasing. This service is particularly valuable in high-pressure projects, distressed situations, and dispute environments.

Typical scope includes: Cost audits, budget reconciliation, forensic quantity take-off, financial exposure mapping.

Strategy, Implementation & Training

For clients building internal capability, we support the implementation of more structured, data-driven ways of working. This includes process design, operating models, and practical guidance that helps teams use BIM and project data more effectively.

Typical scope includes: BIM execution plans, workflow design, team training, tool adoption support.
